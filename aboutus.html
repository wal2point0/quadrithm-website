What is Quadrithm

Four men embarked on a journey to discover something new and acquire new skills. They didn’t know what they would find or who they would become, but they knew they had to return with an artefact that symbolised their achievements on this expedition. Before this artefact could be attained, the four men had to complete arduous missions that would test their capabilities and grit. Little did they know that their lives were going to change forever when they discovered an entity called Quadrithm;
